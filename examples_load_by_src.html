Various Load-by-SRC Examples for jsonToD3.js

The Chart

Things are a bit cluttered. Try clicking on the legend to hide some series. Right click to show just one series.

For details on the points, try hovering the mouse cursor over some of them.

Note also that the translucent points lead to something of a empirical density plot. (So, opacity can, of course, be customized...)

Also note that data is being loaded from some other file in contrast to the examples in examples_inline.html. Tentatively, these have to live on the same site. Although at some point, JSONP-like functionality might be considered.

Code:


<SCATTERPLOT SRC="./examples/hdbRental_Aug2014.json"></SCATTERPLOT>
